About me

A marketer who chose creativity
over convention.

I always knew I was more creative than technical. While others around me were doing IT degrees, I was drawn to ideas — how to grab attention, tell a story, make something go viral.

So I took a leap. I enrolled in a social media marketing course and committed to 12 hours a day for 3 months — learning everything from SEO to paid ads, completely from scratch. That grind got me my first internship at Sportskeeda, and I never looked back.

Since then I've grown audiences, managed influencer campaigns reaching 1 million+ users, written cold email sequences with a 50% open rate, and booked 40+ meetings for clients.
